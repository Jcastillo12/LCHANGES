Cable & Wireless Communications

Cable & Wireless Communications is a full service communications and entertainment provider and delivers market-leading basic and enhanced video, broadband internet and fixed-line telephony to approximately 1.9 million service subscribers (RGUs) represented by almost 980,000 customers as well as mobile voice and data services to 3.4 million mobile subscribers as of September 30, 2019. In addition, through its business division, C&W also operates a state-of-the-art submarine fiber network – the most extensive in the region – connecting over 40 markets. C&W primarily operates under the consumer brands Flow, UTS, Más Móvil, and BTC which are located in the Caribbean, Panama and Bahamas, respectively.
